We made a website together with the help of: Javascript, HTML & CSS.
With this website you can calculate the BMI of someone on the basis of your height and weight.
What to do to calculate your BMI (Body Mass Index): type your height and weight in the fields that say heigt and weight,
if you've done that you click on calculate BMI and you wil get an pop-up on your screen with the result
How does it get calculated? We calculate: weight / (length x length) = BMI.
BTW you need to type the length with a , or a . as example: "1.20" or "1,20"
